Landing Page

The ceremonial entry point. Dark, minimal, intentional. Users are greeted with the core MORPHA philosophy and a quiet invitation to enter.

Active Ritual

Glyphs morphing and breathing in response to sound. Each symbol belongs to a family (Anchor, Flow, Fracture, Void, Echo) with distinct behaviors and meanings.

Sound Response

Different frequencies create different symbol families. Low tones create grounding anchors, high frequencies create fractured tension, balanced sounds create flowing motion.

Silence as Signal

During silence, time stretches. Glyphs persist longer, morphing slows, and the system listens. Silence is not absence—it is presence.

Saving Moments

Long press anywhere to save the current ritual moment. A subtle indicator (◦) appears, and the moment is captured as a seamless looping video.

Mobile Responsive

MORPHA adapts to mobile devices with touch interactions. The full experience translates to smaller screens without losing its contemplative nature.

How to Experience MORPHA:

1. Visit the live demo or run locally
2. Click "enter quietly"
3. Allow microphone access
4. Make sounds, stay silent, or both
5. Long press to save special moments

There is no wrong way—only your way.
